What is Landscaping?

Garden maintenance involves the consistent care and attention needed to keep a garden healthy, vibrant, and visually appealing. This includes tasks like lawn care, which covers mowing, edging, and fertilizing the grass to maintain its lush appearance. Regular weeding is essential to prevent invasive plants from overtaking your garden. Plant care, another critical aspect, involves pruning to remove dead or overgrown parts, watering to ensure adequate moisture, and mulching to retain soil moisture and suppress weeds. Additionally, garden maintenance may include pest control to protect plants from harmful insects and diseases. Overall, garden maintenance ensures that your outdoor space remains a beautiful and inviting environment throughout the year.
