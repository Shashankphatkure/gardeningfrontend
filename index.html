XPC Gardening Services

Hi I’m Dan, I’ve been running XPC Gardening Services for a period of 10 years. I have a wealth of experience going further back under the tutelage of the Royal Horticultural Society Wisley, where I worked for 5 years. My keen passion is planting and watching things grow and flourish. My keen interest in gardening began at a very young age as a pastime I did with family, which later became a passionate profession. I take great pride in my work with a lot of emphasis on productivity and efficiency to ensure that my clients receive the best value for money. I’m a friendly, easy going and meticulous man who enjoys working alongside my clients to help them envisage their dream garden as a reality.
